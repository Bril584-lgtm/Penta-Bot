# Penta-Bot — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 11, 2026**

## 1. What We Collect
Penta-Bot does not store any personal data. Messages sent to the bot are processed in real time and are not logged or retained.

## 2. How It Works
When you use a slash command or @mention the bot, your message is sent to the Anthropic API to generate a response. No message history is stored after the response is delivered.

## 3. Third-Party Services
Penta-Bot uses the following third-party services:
- **Discord API** — to operate within Discord servers
- **Anthropic API (Claude)** — to generate AI responses

Please refer to their respective privacy policies:
- Discord: https://discord.com/privacy
- Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

## 4. Data Retention
Penta-Bot retains no user data. No usernames, IDs, or message content are stored.

## 5. Contact
For privacy concerns, open a ticket in the Pentathletes Discord server.
